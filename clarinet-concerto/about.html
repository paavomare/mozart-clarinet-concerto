About

The aim of the encoding was an exact reproduction of the edition by Franz Giegling, 1977. There were no editorial interventions made. Additional cadenzas by Meyer/Wehle should demonstrate technical possibilities of the providing of an alternative content. This section could be later extended with further suggestions from these editors as well as with cadenzas from earlier authors such as Stanislav Krticka, Jacque Ibert, Karlheinz Stockchausen (cf. Lawson, 1996, pp. 75-76). Another option for a future digital edition would be to merge the both versions for basset- and soprano clarinets.
