The aim of the encoding was an exact reproduction of the edition by Franz Giegling, 1977. Additional cadenzas by Meyer/Wehle are provided for a technical demonstration purpose only. This section could be extended with other suggestions from these editors as well as with cadenzas from earlier authors such as Stanislav Krticka, Jacque Ibert, Karlheinz Stockchausen (cf. Lawson, 1996, pp. 75-76). Another option for a future digital edition would be to merge the versions for basset- and soprano clarinets.
